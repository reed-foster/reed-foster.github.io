About Me


I'm a masters student at MIT studying electrical engineering. I'm interested in computer architecture, reconfigurable computing, analog and digital circuit design, quantum physics, and electromagnetics. My research focuses on the development of superconducting electronics for applications in single-photon detection and cryogenic digital logic.
